Biography

Wouter Van Dooren is professor of Public Administration at the Department of Political Science of the University of Antwerp, Belgium. Before, he was postdoctoral fellow of the Flemish Research Foundation at the KULeuven, seconded expert at the Governance Directorate of the OECD in Paris and researcher at the Public Governance Institute (KULeuven). He also teaches public management at the Antwerp Management School.
